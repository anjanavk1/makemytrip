Experience Overview

Immerse yourself in a vibrant paradise at Dubai Miracle Garden, the world's largest natural flower garden. Spanning over 72,000 square meters, it features over 150 million flowers beautifully arranged in colorful arches, patterns, and myriad shapes.

Marvel at the Guinness World Record-holding rendition of the Emirates Airbus A380, completely covered in flowers. Walk through the heart-shaped pathway, see the giant floral teddy bear, and explore the smurfs village. It's a photographer's dream and a delightful experience for families and nature lovers alike.

The garden offers plenty of food kiosks, souvenir shops, and shaded seating areas to relax and enjoy the scenery.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is it open?

Miracle Garden operates seasonally, typically from October to April. Detailed timings are 09:00 AM - 09:00 PM (Weekdays) and 09:00 AM - 11:00 PM (Weekends).

Is outside food allowed?

No, outside food and beverages are not permitted. There are many dining options inside the garden.
