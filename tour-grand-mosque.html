Tour Overview

Take a day trip from Dubai to the UAE's capital, Abu Dhabi, to visit the magnificent Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. This architectural wonder is one of the largest mosques in the world and a key place of worship for daily prayers.

Admire the mosque's stunning white marble structure, intricate Islamic geometric designs, and reflective pools. Inside, walk on the world's largest hand-knotted carpet and gaze up at the massive Swarovski crystal chandeliers. The sheer scale and beauty of the mosque are breathtaking.

Our guided tour ensures a comfortable journey from Dubai and provides cultural insights into the mosque's history and significance. This is a must-visit cultural landmark for anyone traveling to the UAE.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Strict dress code applies. Men must wear long trousers and shirts with sleeves. Women must cover their hair, arms, and legs. Loose fitting clothing is required. Abayas are provided for women if needed.

Are tattoos allowed?

All tattoos must be covered.

Can we bring food?

Food and drink (except water) are not permitted inside the mosque grounds.
