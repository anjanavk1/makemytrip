Experience Overview

Explore Palm Jumeirah, the world's largest man-made island, known for its glitzy hotels, posh apartment towers, and upmarket global restaurants. Our tour gives you a comprehensive experience of this architectural marvel.

We start with a drive along the "trunk" of the Palm to The Pointe, a waterfront dining and entertainment destination, which offers the perfect vantage point for photos of Atlantis The Palm.

For an elevated experience, we can include tickets to "The View at The Palm," an observation deck on the 52nd floor of The Palm Tower, offering stunning 360-degree views of Palm Jumeirah, the Arabian Gulf, and the Dubai skyline.
