Dubai City Tour

Old Tradition Meets Modern Luxury

Overview

See the best of Dubai in a comprehensive half-day tour. From the historic sights of Dubai Creek, where you'll cross the water in a traditional Abra taxi, to the glittering Gold and Spice Souks. Capture photos of the iconic Burj Al Arab and Atlantis The Palm. Drive down Sheikh Zayed Road to see the Museum of the Future and the majestic Burj Khalifa. This tour is the perfect introduction to the city.

  • Gold & Spice Souks
