This is the finalized, comprehensive **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** for **Zorx Travel Agency**. It is designed to be handed directly to a UI/UX designer and Web Developer to build a high-converting, animated MVP.

---

# PRD: Zorx Travel Agency Digital Platform

**Project Status:** MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

**Niche:** Luxury Tourism & Curated Arabian Adventures

**Focus:** Lead Generation & WhatsApp Conversion

---

## 1. Brand & Aesthetic Identity

* **Brand Name:** ZORX TRAVEL AGENCY
* **Core Value Prop:** "Your Trusted Partner for Curated Arabian Adventures."
* **Color Palette:**
* **Primary (Sky Blue):** #00AEEF (Main buttons, icons, accents).
* **Secondary (Pure White):** #FFFFFF (Backgrounds, clean spacing).
* **Text (Deep Navy):** #012169 (Headlines and readability).


* **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., **Montserrat** or **Poppins**).
* **Visual Style:** High-end, airy, and professional. Uses high-resolution photography with plenty of white space.

---

## 2. Animation & Interaction (The "Premium" Feel)

* **Hero Section:** The City Skyline image should have a subtle **Ken Burns effect** (slow zoom-in).
* **Scroll Reveal:** Use a staggered fade-in for tour cards (the first card appears, then the second 100ms later).
* **Sticky Navigation:** The header should be transparent on the hero section and turn solid White/Sky Blue upon scrolling.
* **Micro-interactions:** Buttons should have a slight "lift" or glow effect when hovered.

---

## 3. Site Architecture (4-Page MVP)

### A. Homepage (The "Hook")

* **Hero Section:** * **Background:** High-res Dubai/City Skyline.
* **Text:** Logo (Zorx Travel) + Tagline + "Explore the Best of the Emirates."
* **CTA:** "Browse Packages" (Smooth scroll to grid).


* **Experience Categories:** Animated icons for: *Desert Safari, City Tours, Attraction Tickets.*
* **Social Proof:** A "Trust Bar" with star ratings from Google and TripAdvisor.
* **Featured Tours:** A 3-card grid of top-selling packages.

### B. Packages / Listing Page (The "Menu")

* **Header:** Simple Sky Blue banner.
* **Grid Layout:** 3-column layout (Mobile: 1-column).
* **Card Anatomy:**
* High-res image.
* Badge: "Best Seller" or "Most Popular."
* Title & Short Lorem Ipsum description.
* Price Label: "Starting from $X."
* **CTA:** "View Package."



### C. Individual Tour Page (The "Sales Pitch")

* **Visuals:** A 3-4 image gallery/slider.
* **Left Column:** * **Description:** Lorem Ipsum rich text.
* **Highlights:** Bullet points (e.g., "Hotel Pickup," "Expert Guide").
* **Itinerary:** A vertical timeline layout.


* **Right Column (Sticky Widget):**
* Clean white box containing a date picker and guest count.
* **Primary CTA:** "Enquire on WhatsApp" (Green button).
* **Secondary CTA:** "Get Custom Quote" (Contact form link).



### D. Contact & Custom Enquiry

* **Form:** Name, Email, WhatsApp Number, and "Custom Requirements" text area.
* **Visuals:** Integrated Google Map and office hours.

---

## 4. Technical Requirements & Lead Flow

| Feature | Specification |
| --- | --- |
| **Lead Flow** | All "Book Now" buttons trigger a WhatsApp message redirect. |
| **WhatsApp Logic** | Pre-fills text: *"Hi Zorx, I'm interested in the [Tour Name]. Please send details."* |
| **Optimization** | 90+ PageSpeed score (WebP images, minified CSS). |
| **Mobile Design** | **Mobile-First.** A sticky bottom bar with a single "Chat with Expert" button. |
| **No Cart** | No payment gateway or cart functionality required for this version. |

---

## 5. Content Structure (Lorem Ipsum)

* **Tour Titles:** "Premium Desert Safari," "Dubai City Lights Tour," "Abu Dhabi Grand Mosque Visit."
* **Descriptions:** 2-3 paragraphs of "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..." to demonstrate layout.
* **About Us:** A 150-word "Our Story" section using placeholder text.
