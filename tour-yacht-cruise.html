Experience Overview

Feel like a VIP as you step aboard our luxury yacht docked in the glittering Dubai Marina. Cruise past the impressive skyline, iconic architecture, and luxury waterfront residences.

Glide through the Dubai Marina Canal, passing beneath picturesque bridges and out towards Blue Waters Island, home to Ain Dubai, the world's largest ferris wheel. Sail past Jumeirah Beach Residence (JBR) and marvel at the Palm Jumeirah and Atlantis The Palm from the open sea.

Relax on the spacious deck, soak up the sun, or chill in the air-conditioned lounge. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just want to see Dubai from a different perspective, this yacht cruise offers the perfect blend of relaxation and sightseeing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my ID?

Yes, as per coast guard regulations, all guests must bring their original Passport or Emirates ID.

Can we swim?

Swimming is allowed on private charters during daylight hours, weather permitting. Please inform us in advance.

Will I get seasick?

The waters around the Marina and Palm are generally very calm. However, if you are prone to motion sickness, we recommend taking medication beforehand.
