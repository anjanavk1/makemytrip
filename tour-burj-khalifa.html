Experience Overview

No trip to Dubai is complete without visiting the iconic Burj Khalifa. Soar to the sky on the world's fastest double-deck elevators, cruising at 10m/s. Watch the horizon sweep by through floor-to-ceiling glass walls as you ascend to the observation deck on level 124.

Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of the world's city grid, desert, and ocean. Step out onto the public outdoor observation terrace overlooking the ever-growing skyline. Continue your visit to level 125, designed as a tribute to Arab arts and culture.

Using advanced telescopes, you can zoom in and learn about landmarks below. Visit during sunset for the most spectacular views, seeing the city transition from day to twinkling night.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit?

Sunset hours are the most popular to see both day and night views. However, early morning offers clearer views with less haze.

Can I bring a bag?

Large bags and luggage must be stored in the Secure Baggage Area near the entrance. Small handbags are allowed.

Is there a time limit for the visit?

Most visitors spend about 90 minutes. However, you can stay as long as you like once you've entered (up to closing time).
