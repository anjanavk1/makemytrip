Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a tour?

Simply click the "Enquire on WhatsApp" button on any tour page, or fill out our contact form. We'll respond with availability and booking details within minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided after booking confirmation.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries. Contact us with your requirements and we'll design the perfect experience for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the tour. Full refund guaranteed. Contact us for more details.

Do you provide hotel pickup?

Yes! Most of our tours include complimentary hotel pickup and drop-off from anywhere in Dubai. Specific details are mentioned in each tour description.
