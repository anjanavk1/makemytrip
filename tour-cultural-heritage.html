Experience Overview

Step back in time and explore the roots of Dubai. Our Cultural Heritage Tour starts in the Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood (Bastakiya), where you can wander through narrow lanes lined with traditional wind-tower houses.

Visit the Dubai Museum housed in the Al Fahidi Fort to understand the city's transformation from a fishing village to a global metropolis. Then, experience the charm of local transport with a ride on a traditional Abra across Dubai Creek.

On the Deira side, get lost in the bustling alleyways of the Gold Souk and Spice Souk. Smell the exotic spices and haggle for souvenirs. This tour offers a fascinating contrast to the modern glitz of New Dubai.
