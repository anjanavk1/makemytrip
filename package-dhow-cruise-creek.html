Dubai Creek Dhow Cruise

Dinner Under the Stars

Overview

Step aboard a traditional wooden Dhow boat for a 2-hour evening cruise along the historic Dubai Creek. Enjoy a delicious international buffet dinner while gliding past the illuminated souks, heritage sites, and modern architecture of old Dubai. The evening includes live entertainment, such as the mesmerizing Tanoura dance show, making it a perfect night out for families and couples.
