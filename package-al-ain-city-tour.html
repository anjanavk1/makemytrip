Al Ain City Tour

The Garden City of the UAE

Overview

Escape the hustle of Dubai and visit the serene 'Garden City' of Al Ain. Known for its greenery, natural springs, and rich history, Al Ain is a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the Sheikh Zayed Palace Museum, explore the lush Al Ain Oasis, see the ancient Falaj irrigation system, and drive up Jebel Hafeet mountain for panoramic views. You'll also visit the famous Camel Market, the last of its kind in the UAE.

Tour Highlights

  • Al Ain Oasis
  • Sheikh Zayed Palace Museum
  • Jebel Hafeet Mountain
  • Green Mubazzarah
  • Camel Market
  • Al Jahili Fort
