Premium Evening Desert Safari

The Ultimate Arabian Adventure

Overview

Experience the magic of the Arabian desert with our Premium Evening Desert Safari. This 5-6 hour adventure combines thrill, culture, and cuisine. Start with an adrenaline-pumping dune bashing session in a 4x4 Land Cruiser, followed by a mesmerizing sunset photo stop. At our traditional Bedouin camp, enjoy camel riding, henna painting, and live entertainment including Tanoura and Belly dancing. The evening concludes with a lavish 5-star international BBQ buffet dinner under the starlit sky.
