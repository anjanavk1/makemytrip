Morning Desert Safari

Start Your Day with Adventure

Overview

Experience the beauty of the desert in the early morning light. The Morning Desert Safari is perfect for those who want to experience dune bashing but have other plans for the evening. Enjoy a thrilling drive across the red sand dunes, try sandboarding down the slopes, and experience a short camel ride. This 4-hour tour focuses on the adventure aspect of the desert.

Tour Highlights

  • Morning Red Dune Bashing (45 mins)
  • Sandboarding
  • Camel Ride
  • Photo Opportunities
  • Water & Soft Drinks
